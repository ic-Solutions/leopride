Guiding you towards your DREAM CAREER

NEET Advisor provides a total career counselling where we have come together to help, guide and counsel students in every aspect from making the right career choice, choosing the right course/institution and even help you settle down in new environs !

NEET Advisor has a competent team of Experienced counsellors which focus on providing the best Educational Guidance and ensures the right input is imparted to students. We are a contemporary Career Counselling company incorporated to provide exclusive and innovative Career solutions.

Team In Action

We are a team of young professionals dedicated to equip students with the clarity, courage and determination to follow their dreams. we are very much passionate to add value in education sector and to establish the world class career counselling services in India. Our team is also working with top notch schools, colleges and coaching institutes to promote their academic programs across India by conducting various career counselling seminars/workshops. we have positively impacted 50,000+ students and helped them in every aspect from making the right career choice, choosing the right course/institution based on their aptitude, interests and personality.
